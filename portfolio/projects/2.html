MuzakMaker

Requirements

The objective of this project was to develop a dynamic and user-friendly webpage for streaming music, featuring both vertical and horizontal sliders to enhance the user's experience. This endeavor involved a systematic approach that contributed to the successful creation of the webpage.

The development phase involved the implementation of the webpage and it's subsequent design, including the integration of audio streaming functions and the incorporation of interactive vertical and horizontal sliders, allowing users to explore music playlists.
