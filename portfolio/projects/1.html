In & Out Delivery Service

Project Requirements

To develop this website, I initiated the process by crafting a mockup. Although I had a clear understanding of my project requirements, the mockup was essential in providing a concrete direction for my webpage.

With the mockup as my guide, I proceeded to structure all the elements of my webpage using HTML5. Once the foundational elements were in place, I leveraged the power of CSS3 to infuse a polished and professional aesthetic, aligning with the design standards set by In & Out.

Upon completing the visual design, I transitioned to the development of interactivity. JavaScript played a pivotal role in enhancing user engagement and creating a dynamic experience for visitors to the website.
