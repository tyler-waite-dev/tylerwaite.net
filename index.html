About Me

Hello there! My name is Tyler, and I am a Web Developer. I attended Arizona State University for Internet & Web Development (with a graduation date of December 2023), but I also took quite a few classes in Software Engineering. This complements my Associate’s Degree in Computer Information Systems, and prior CompTIA A+ and Security+ certifications.

In addition to my numerous years in higher education, I have spent six years working as a line cook, where I worked on honing a hard work ethic and attention to detail. My curiosity for food goes beyond just work – you’ll often catch me cooking new foods or making big batches of hibiscus in my home kitchen. Hailing from Upstate NY, I relocated to San Diego in 2020 in pursuit of opportunity and *sunshine*.
